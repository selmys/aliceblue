General

I can't get the game to run! (1 hint)

I

Consult README.txt or Alice-Manual.pdf for help with getting the game to work on your system.

I can't move! (2 hints)

I

Have you tried the 'exits' command?

II

Althought the compass directions will not work, their abreviations still do because they are also short for nowhere, somewhere, elsewhere and wherever, in which directions you are free to travel.

What am I supposed to do? (4 hints)

I

Explore, experiment.

II

There are twelve environments to discover. Some of them will quickly dead-end initially, come back to these ones later.

III

At any given time there is one that can be completed to unlock the following one.

IV

At the begining of the game this is the Bedroom.

How do I talk to my grandmother? (2 hints)

I

First you need to remember her from @My Bedroom.

II

You will then be able to find her in the Field of Flowers.

The screen has turned white and there are no more keywords! (4 hints)

I

That's as complete as this memory is going to get...

II

...for now.

III

Use 'nowhere', 'somewhere', 'elsewhere', and 'wherever' to navigate to another incomplete memory.

IV

There is no way to undo entering an amber keyword apart from restoring a previously saved game, so make sure you have finished exploring before doing so.

How do I open the door? (3 hints)

I

You don't need to unlock this door, but you do need to find a key.

II

III

Points

How can I get a perfect score? (6 hints)

I

Talk to grandma's ghost.

II

Make sure you find all the room names before you trigger the associated memory.

III

Find all instances of REMEMBER, SUNLIGHT, and GRANDMOTHER.

IV

Eat the parsley.

V

Type in what's missing in the Field of Flowers.

How can I score above 124? (1 hints)

I

In addition to the above, explore very thoroughly.

Why is my final score not a round number? (1 hint)

I

There are many actions in the game that award a small fraction of a point. The only way to be sure to get all of these is to explore everything.
